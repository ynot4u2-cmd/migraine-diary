Privacy Policy – Migraine Diary

Last updated: 2026-01-04

Overview

Migraine Diary is an offline application designed to help users log migraine episodes for personal reference. The app does not collect or transmit personal data.

Data Collection

Migraine Diary does not collect, transmit, or share personal data.

  • No user accounts
  • No internet access
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising

Data Storage

All data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device only.

  • Data never leaves the device
  • No external backups
  • The developer has no access to user data

Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services or SDKs that collect data.

Medical Disclaimer

This app does not provide medical advice. Consult a qualified healthcare professional if needed.

Children’s Privacy

The app is not specifically targeted at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Contact the developer via the Google Play listing.
